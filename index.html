About Me

I'm Noah S Hardy, a developer who finds meaning in the small, intentional project. Not the grand platform, not the funded startup — the curious experiment, the useful little tool, the thing that exists because I needed it to.

My work tends to live at the edges of things: between design and engineering, between useful and beautiful, between finished and forever-in-progress.

I build in public and in private, ship things that might fail, and learn better by making than by reading about making.
